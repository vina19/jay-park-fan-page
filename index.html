Bio

Jay park is a successful musician, actor, entrepreneur, producer, and charitable man. He is also a CEO of record labels called AOMG and H1GHRMUSIC. He is an advocate for the fight against AIDS and campaigned for Breast Cancer Awareness. He set up an international record label H1GHRMUSIC to supports international mainstream and underground artists.
